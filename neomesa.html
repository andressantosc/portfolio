Neomesa

The company

Neomesa was created when I was 20 years old as a holding company for future projects. The first project was the regional development of a restaurant franchise from Spain called 100 Montaditos. After months of negotiations with the parent company, they decided to give us the exclusive rights to operate the franchise in northern Mexico.

After getting the rights, Neomesa had a funding round to be able to carry out this project. We received funding for the first two restaurants. I travelled to Miami to begin my 4-week training and certified myself in restaurant management. Since then, Neomesa has had no other partners or funding rounds and has moved on to form a joint venture called Aguila Soul and has participated in the expansion of the Mexican franchise "La Borra del Caf√©".

The project

I took management of the project from the start. Since I was only 20 years old at the time, I had to learn as much as I could along the way. I supervised construction of the two restaurants, learning about architecture, civil engineering, project management, and more.

Once the restaurant was opened, I was in charge of recruiting and leading employees. I oversaw purchasing, marketing, quality control, inventory management, customer service, accounting, franchise relationships, among other tasks.
