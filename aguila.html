Aguila Soul

The project

Aguila Soul is Neomesa's first joint venture. It was created as a parternship with Grupo Culinaria, one of the most succesful and prestigious restaurant groups of Mexico, with over 20 years of experience in the industry. The new venture has created an innovative casual mexican restaurant concept called Aguila Soul and is currently operating it's first restaurant.

My role in the project was in negotiating a deal with Grupo Culinaria and approving and developing the concept and design of the restaurant.
